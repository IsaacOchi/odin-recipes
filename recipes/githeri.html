Githeri Recipe

Githeri Recipe for Two

This dish is popular amongst many Kenyan Households. It can be taken for lunch or supper and is very filling and nutritious.

Ingredients used

  • 1 Cup cooked Maize
  • 7 Tablespoons cooking oil of your choice
  • 2 cups cooked beans
  • 2 Large potatoes
  • 1 Large onions
  • 2 Large tomatoes
  • 1 Bunch of dhania
  • Salt to taste

How to Prepare

  1. Prepare your Ingredients. Finely chop the tomatoes, onion and dhania. Peel the potatoes and clean them.
  2. Place your sufuria under medium heat. Add 7 tablespoons of cooking oil and the onions. Cook till golden brown then add the tomatoes.
  3. Once the tomatoes have cooked, add your potatoes and cook for about 4min. We don't want the potatoes to fully cook. This will prevent them from cracking as we continue cooking.
  4. Next add the cooked maize and beans. Stir for 2mins then add 1 more cup of water. Cook until the potatoes are ready and the water has reduced to desired level.
  5. Add the dhania and cook for 2mins. Thats it. Our githeri is ready.
