Rice Beans Recipe

Rice and beans is a nutritious and filling dish that is popular in many cultures. It is a great source of protein and can be enjoyed as a main course or a side dish.

This recipe is easy to follow and can be made in under an hour. It is a great option for lunch or dinner.

Rice and beans is not only tasty but also healthy, making it a perfect meal for the whole family. Enjoy this delightful dish with your favorite sides!

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Servings

This recipe serves 2 people.

Difficulty Level

Easy

Nutrition

Calories: 350

Protein: 15g

Carbohydrates: 60g

Fat: 5g

Fiber: 10g

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pre-boiled beans
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 bunch of spinach
  • 1 table spoon of salt
  • Cooking oil
  • 1 large bulb onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of corriander

Preparation

  1. Chop onions, tomatoes and corriander. Wash the rice and soak it in a dish
  2. In a pot over medium heat, add 5 tablespoons of oil, the chopped onions. Cook until golden brown.
  3. Add all the chopped tomatoes. Cook until they become soft.
  4. Add the cooked beans and stir. Add salt to taste.
  5. Add two cups of water and let the mixture simmer stirring occasionally. Cook for 15 mins.
  6. Add the chopped corriander for garnishing. Set aside
  7. In a pot over medium heat, add 2 cups of water, salt to taste and 2 table spoons of oil.
  8. Once the water boils, add the soaked rice. Stir and let it cook
  9. Serve and enjoy
