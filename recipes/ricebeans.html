Rice Beans Recipe

Rice Beans Recipe for Two

This dish is popular amongst many Kenyan Households. It can be taken for lunch or supper and is very filling and nutritious.

Ingredients used

  • 1 cup of pishori rice
  • 8 Tablespoons cooking oil of your choice
  • 2 cups of cooked beans
  • 1 Medium sized onions
  • 2 Large tomatoes
  • 1 Bunch of dhania
  • Salt to taste

How to Prepare

  1. Prepare your Ingredients. Finely chop the tomatoes, onions and dhania. Wash the rice and place it in a bowl with water.
  2. We will start with the beans. Place your sufuria under medium heat. Add 3 tablespoons of cooking oil and the onions. Cook till golden brown then add the tomatoes.
  3. Once the tomatoes have cooked, add your beans and cook for 2mins. Add 1/2 cup of water. Bring the mixture to boil. If you want more soup for your beans, add more water. Add salt to taste. Add the chopped dhania and cook for 2mins. Done. The beans are ready. Set aside and lets make the rice.
  4. Place your pan under medium heat. Add 6 tablespoons of oil and one cup of water. Add salt to taste. Bring to water to boil.
  5. Sieve the rice and add it to the boiling water. Cook the rice on medium heat until 3/4 of the water has been absorbed. Cover the rice completely and reduce the heat to low.
  6. Check regularly until the water is fully absorbed and the rice is soft. Thats it. Our rice and beans is ready.
