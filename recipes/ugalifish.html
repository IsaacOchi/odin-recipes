Ugali and Fish Recipe

Ugali and fish is a traditional dish from the coastal region of Kenya. It is a delicious combination of maize flour and fish, often served with vegetables or stew.

This recipe is easy to follow and can be made in under an hour. It is a great option for lunch or dinner.

Ugali and fish is not only tasty but also nutritious, making it a perfect meal for the whole family. Enjoy this delightful dish with your favorite sides!

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 80 minutes

Total Time: 90 minutes

Servings

This recipe serves 2 people.

Difficulty Level

Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 1 medium tilapia size Fish
  • 1 bunch of spinach
  • 1 table spoon of salt
  • Cooking oil
  • 1 large bulb onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of corriander
  • 1 cube fish boulion

Preparation

  1. Chop spinnach, onions, tomatoes and corriander. Slice the fish in half.
  2. In a large pan over medium heat, add oil enough to deep fry the fish. Once the oil is hot, deep fry the fish untill its golden brown and crispy. Set aside
  3. In a pot over medium heat, add 5 tablespoons of oil, 3/4 of the chopped onions. Cook until golden brown.
  4. Add all the chopped tomatoes. Cook until they become soft.
  5. Add fish boulion in 1 cup of water. Pour it ovet the tomatoes and let it cook for 5 mins
  6. Add the two pieces of fish. Add salt to taste. Let it cook untill a small amount of the broth is visible.
  7. Add the chopped corriander for garnishing. Set aside
  8. In a pan over medium heat. Add 3 tablespoons of oil and the remaining onions.
  9. Cook until golden brown.
  10. Add the chopped spinnach and cook for 5 min stirring continously. Add salt to taste. Set aside
  11. In a pot over medium heat, add 1 and 1/2 cups of water.
  12. Once the water boils, add the flour in portions, kneading with a wooden spoon untill a medium-hard dough is formed.
  13. At this point, knead until no dry patches of flour are visible. Shape the dough in to a circular shape.
  14. Cover the pot and let it cook for 5 mins. Its ready when it gives off a sweat baked smell. Set aside
  15. Serve and enjoy
