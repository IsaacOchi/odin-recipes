Ugali Mayai Recipe

Quick Chilli Ugali Mayai Recipe for Two

This dish is popular amongst many Kenyan Households. It can be taken for lunch or supper and is very filling and nutritious.

Ingredients used

  • 3 cups Maize Flour
  • 6 Tablespoons cooking oil of your choice
  • 4 Eggs
  • 10 Sticks of chopped Kales
  • 2 Medium sized onions
  • 2 Large tomatoes
  • 1 Bunch of dhania
  • 3 Large green chillies
  • Salt to taste

How to Prepare

  1. Prepare your Ingredients. Finely chop the kales, tomatoes, onion, dhania and chillies. Also, Crack all the eggs
  2. We will start with the greens. Place your sufuria under medium heat. Add 3 tablespoons of cooking oil and half the onions. Cook till golden brown then add half of the tomatoes.
  3. Once the tomatoes have cooked, add your kales and cook for about 7min add salt to taste. Put aside and next we move on to the eggs.
  4. Place your pan under medium heat. Add 3 tablespoons of cooking and the remaining onions. Cook till golden brown. the add the remaining tomatoes and chillies.
  5. Once the tomatoes and chillies are ready, whisk the eggs and add them inside the mixture. Stir continuously for about 10min to get a grain like mixture.
  6. Add the dhania and cook for 1min. Done. The eggs are ready. Set aside and next we move on to the Ugali
  7. Place your sufuria under medium heat. Add 2 cups of water. Bring the water to boil. Add the flour in small chunks. Mix using a mwiko<. Once the mixture starts to solidify, stop adding flour. Stir until it bakes completely. You will know this through the smell./li>
  8. Using the mwiko, form a large ball let it cook for 1min. Transfer the ball onto a plate.
  9. Thats it!. Our Chill eggs and ugali is ready.
